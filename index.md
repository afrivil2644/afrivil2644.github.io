## 📌 About this project

Zan turns your voice into text. The app sits in your menu bar. You press a key, speak, and the app writes your words. It uses artificial intelligence to fix your grammar and change your text style. Your data stays on your machine. The app does not track your habits.

## 🛠️ System requirements

Your computer needs a few things to run this tool well:

* Windows 10 or Windows 11.
* A stable internet connection for the speech processing.
* Access to a computer microphone.
* At least 200MB of free disk space.

## 🎯 Getting started

Open the app to see the icon in your system tray. Click the icon to open the main menu. You will see a button to link your OpenAI account. This key allows the app to process your voice files. Paste your key into the box and press save.

To start dictation, press the trigger key on your keyboard. A small light will signify that the app listens. Speak clearly into your microphone. Press the same key again to stop. The app will turn your audio into text. It will paste the result directly into your active window.

## ⚙️ Configuration options

You can change how the app works in the settings menu:

* Trigger key: Pick a button on your keyboard to start and stop dictation.
* Language: Choose the language you speak so the app understands you better.
* Audio input: Select your microphone from a list of devices.
* Text rules: Create presets to change your tone. You can make text formal, short, or casual.

## 📝 Tips for success

* Use a quiet room for the best results.
* Speak at a normal pace.
* Pause briefly between sentences.
* Keep your microphone close to your mouth.
* Check your internet connection if the app feels slow.

## 🛡️ Privacy and safety

This app protects your privacy. It does not send your voice data to public clouds for long-term storage. The app uses local processing to handle tasks. It only communicates with your chosen provider to finish the text transforms. No one sees your personal notes or private documents. You own all output generated by the software. 

## ❓ Common questions

**Does the app work offline?**
The app requires an internet connection to process speech. Local files remain on your device, but the translation happen on a remote server.

**Can I use a custom key for my account?**
Yes. Use the settings menu to update your key at any time.

**Where does the app save my voice data?**
The app deletes your audio files after the text generation finishes. It does not save recordings on your hard drive.

**How do I delete the app?**
Open your Windows Control Panel. Select Programs and Features. Find the app in the list and click uninstall.

**Who makes this software?**
This project exists thanks to community members who value speed and privacy in their tools. It relies on open standards like Whisper for high accuracy. 

**Does it work with other apps?**
Zan works in any text box. Use it in your web browser, document editors, or email clients. It acts like a keyboard when it inserts text into your chosen app.
